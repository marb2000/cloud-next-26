# ⚡️ MemoTattoo: Your AI Flashcard Adventure Starts Here!

Welcome, Architect of Knowledge! You've successfully deployed your MemoTattoo ecosystem. Now it's time to actually use it. This guide will walk you through your first steps with the **Web Console** and the **Mobile App**.

---

## 🕸 Part 1: The Command Center (Web Console)

The Web Console is where you, the Admin, can create premium content, moderate the library, and manage users.

### 1. Login with your Admin superpowers
Use the email and password you configured in `sync-config.json`. Once inside, you'll see a sleek dashboard showing your platform's activity.

### 2. The Flashcard Studio: AI Brainstorming
Head over to the **Flashcard Studio**. This is the heart of your content creation.
*   **The Vibe**: Enter a topic like *"Essential Italian for Coffee Lovers"*.
*   **The Magic**: Hit "Brainstorm". Gemini will generate a list of concepts (terms and definitions) tailored to your topic.
*   **The Art**: For each concept, click "Generate Image". Vertex AI will paint a custom illustration.
    *   *Pro Tip*: Don't like the result? Use the "Refine Artwork" prompt to say something like *"Make it more futuristic and neon"*!

### 3. Moderation: The Quality Gate
When users (or you) submit a deck to the Global Library, it lands in the **Moderation Queue**.
*   Review concepts for accuracy and "vibes".
*   Click **Approve** to push it live to all mobile users.
*   Rejected decks go back to the user as a private draft.

---

## 📱 Part 2: The Player's Journey (Mobile App)

This is where the learning happens. The mobile app is designed for high-engagement "Game Sessions".

### 1. Your Personal Library ("My Decks")
Every deck you create is yours first. You can practice them privately as many times as you want.
*   **Game On**: Tap the "Play" icon on any deck.
*   **The Experience**: Swipe through cards, master the concepts, and see your score climb. The app uses AI-driven SRS (Spaced Repetition) to keep your memory sharp.

### 2. Going Global ("Public Decks")
Ready to share your genius? Long-press a deck in your library and select **"Publish to Global Library"**.
*   Your deck will disappear from your private list and show up as **"Moderating"**.
*   Once an admin (you, on the web!) approves it, it will join the **Public Decks** tab for the whole world to enjoy.

### 3. The Energy Economy
Notice the ⚡️ Bolts in your profile?
*   Every AI generation costs Energy Bolts.
*   Free users get a daily top-up.
*   Submitting high-quality decks that get approved is a great way to earn respect (and maybe one day, premium rewards!).

---

## 🚀 Pro Tips for Your First Deck
1.  **Start Small**: Try a 5-card deck about your favorite hobby.
2.  **Experiment with Art**: Use the "Global Art Direction" field in the Studio to give the whole deck a consistent style (e.g., *"Studio Ghibli aesthetic"* or *"3D claymation"*).
3.  **Review Daily**: The app will remind you when it's time to review your cards. Consistency is key!
